    Discover

    Given a business goal or focus area, "Discovery" is the investigation of finding all the human problems we can solve that will help us achieve the business goal. I typically do this by:

    • Business stakeholder interviews
    • Talking to teams who would know more than me
    • Watching targeted user recordings
    • Analysing quantitaitve user behaviour
    • Interviewing users
    • Competitive & comparitve research
    • Targeted surveys
